Medtronic Hackathon 2nd Place

Honored to have placed 2nd at the Medtronic Hackathon with my teammates. Our concept builds upon Medtronic’s MyCareLink platform by introducing new features that empower patients with greater access to their health data, helping them better understand their condition, take proactive steps toward healthier habits, and strengthen communication with their care teams.

This hackathon pushed me to think beyond engineering and consider the full patient experience, clinical workflows, and the importance of designing with end patients in mind.
